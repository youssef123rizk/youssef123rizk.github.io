Black crowned-crane

Black crowned-crane gets its name from its dark grey and black plumage, and the golden crown of feathers at the top of its head. Black crowned-cranes live in freshwater wetlands and the edges of lakes, ponds and rivers.

Proboscis Monkey

The proboscis monkey or long-nosed monkey is a kind of monkey with a reddish-brown colour. They live in groups composed of some females and one male. Groups stay together during the day and travel together.
