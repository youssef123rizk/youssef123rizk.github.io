Amazing Facts

The red-billed hornbills are found in savannah. They are split into different species. They have long tails, a long and curved red bill that lacks a casque. They feed mainly on the ground, seeds, insects and fruit!
Parrots are found in most warm and tropical regions. The specific features of parrots include a strong curved bill and strong legs. They have four toes, two toes pointing forward and two toes pointing backward. They are some of the most intelligent birds!
The proboscis monkey or long-nosed monkey is a kind of monkey with a reddish-brown colour. They live in groups composed of some females and one male. Groups stay together during the day and travel together!
Black crowned-crane gets its name from its dark grey and black plumage, and the golden crown of feathers at the top of its head. Black crowned-cranes live in freshwater wetlands and the edges of lakes, ponds and rivers!
